Frequently Asked Questions

How does the lifetime access contest work?

One lucky person who joins our waitlist will be randomly selected to win completely free lifetime access to HumGod. This includes all premium features, unlimited tracks, and no subscription fees - forever. The winner will be announced when we launch.

When will HumGod be available?

We're targeting a beta launch in Q3 2025, with full public release in Q4 2025. Waitlist members will get early access before the public launch.

What if I'm not a musician?

HumGod is designed for everyone! Whether you're a professional musician, content creator, hobbyist, or just someone who loves music - HumGod makes music creation accessible to all skill levels.

Will my data be safe?

Absolutely. We take privacy seriously and will never share your personal information. We only use your email to send important updates about HumGod.

Can I share this with friends?

Yes! Feel free to share the waitlist with friends, family, or anyone who might be interested. The more people who join, the better we can make HumGod for everyone.

What if I don't win the contest?

Don't worry! All waitlist members become founders with early beta access, exclusive community status, special launch pricing, and the opportunity to help shape HumGod's development. You'll be part of the core team that builds the future of music creation.
